Historical Sites of Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is dotted with ancient forts, temples, and monasteries. Kangra Fort is one of the oldest forts in India, offering stunning views and historical depth. Key Monastery in Spiti Valley is a beautiful Buddhist monastery over 1,000 years old, built in the Tibetan style. Bhuri Singh Museum in Chamba showcases manuscripts, paintings, and cultural artifacts.
