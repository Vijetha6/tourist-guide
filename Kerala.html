KERALA

Kerala is a state in the southwestern region of India, famous for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, and rich cultural traditions.

Geography and Climate

  • Location: Kerala is located along the Malabar Coast, bordered by Karnataka to the north and Tamil Nadu to the east.
  • Capital: Thiruvananthapuram (also known as Trivandrum) is the capital of Kerala.
  • Terrain: Kerala features coastal plains, rolling hills, and the Western Ghats, with a tropical monsoon climate.

Culture and Heritage

  • Language: Malayalam is the official and most widely spoken language in Kerala.
  • Cuisine: Known for coconut-based dishes and spices; popular dishes include appam, puttu, and fish curry.
  • Festivals: Major festivals include Onam, Vishu, and Thrissur Pooram.
