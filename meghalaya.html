MEGHALAYA

Meghalaya is a state in northeastern India, known for its scenic beauty, rainfall, caves, and indigenous culture.

Geography and Climate

  • Location: Meghalaya is located in the northeastern part of India, bordered by Assam and Bangladesh.
  • Capital: Shillong is the capital city of Meghalaya and is often referred to as the "Scotland of the East."
  • Terrain: The state features rolling hills, lush forests, waterfalls, and is part of the Meghalaya Plateau.

Culture and Heritage

  • Language: Khasi, Garo, and English are the major languages spoken in Meghalaya.
  • Cuisine: The food includes rice, meat, and bamboo shoot dishes, with favorites like Jadoh and Dohneiiong.
  • Festivals: Major festivals include Wangala (harvest festival of the Garos) and Shad Suk Mynsiem (Khasi spring festival).
