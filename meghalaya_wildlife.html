Wildlife of Meghalaya

Meghalaya is famous for natural attractions such as Mawsmai Caves, Elephant Falls, and the root bridges of Cherrapunji and Mawlynnong.

Balpakram National Park

This park in the Garo Hills is often called the “Land of Spirits.” It's home to rare species like the red panda, wild water buffalo, and golden cat.

Nokrek National Park

A UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, Nokrek is known for its rich biodiversity and is believed to be the home of the rare citrus fruit species Citrus indica.
