Beaches

Karnataka's coastline offers beautiful beaches, such as Gokarna and Murudeshwar.

Gokarna Beach

Known for its peaceful vibe and scenic beauty, Gokarna has multiple beaches like Om Beach, Kudle Beach, and Paradise Beach. It's a perfect getaway for spiritual seekers and nature lovers.

Murudeshwar Beach

Famous for the towering Shiva statue and Murudeshwar Temple, this beach offers a divine view of the Arabian Sea and is great for a spiritual beach experience.
