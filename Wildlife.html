Wildlife

Karnataka has several wildlife sanctuaries and national parks, including Bandipur National Park and Nagarhole National Park.

Bandipur National Park

Bandipur is one of the premier tiger reserves in India and forms part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. Located in the Western Ghats, it is known for its large population of elephants, deer, leopards, and tigers. The park offers safari experiences and is a key destination for wildlife lovers and photographers.

Nagarhole National Park

Nagarhole is a lush forest filled with streams, valleys, and rich wildlife. It is home to Bengal tigers, Asian elephants, wild dogs, and over 270 species of birds. The park is part of the Nilgiri Biosphere and provides thrilling jeep and boat safaris.

Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary

Located in North Karnataka, Dandeli is known for its dense forests, adventure activities, and diverse wildlife. It is home to black panthers, crocodiles, bison, and hornbills. Dandeli is also popular for river rafting and trekking.
