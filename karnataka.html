KARNATAKA

Karnataka is a state in southwestern India, known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse geography, and vibrant cities.

Geography and Climate

  • Location: Karnataka is situated in the southwestern part of India, bordered by Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala.
  • Capital: Bengaluru (also known as Bangalore) is the capital city of Karnataka.
  • Terrain: Karnataka's terrain varies from coastal plains to hills and mountains, including the Western Ghats.

Culture and Heritage

  • Language: Kannada is the official language of Karnataka, and it's also the most widely spoken language in the state.
  • Cuisine: Karnataka's cuisine is known for its use of rice, lentils, and spices, with popular dishes like bisi bele bath, akki roti, and holige.
  • Festivals: Karnataka celebrates various festivals, including Ugadi (Kannada New Year), Makara Sankranti, and Karaga.
