Wildlife of Kerala

The state is a biodiversity hotspot, home to numerous wildlife sanctuaries and national parks. The Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary in Thekkady is famous for elephants and scenic boat rides. Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary in the Western Ghats shelters leopards, bison, and rare birds. Silent Valley National Park protects pristine rainforest and endangered species like the lion-tailed macaque.
