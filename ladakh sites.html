Historical Sites of Ladakh

Ladakh has a rich Buddhist heritage visible in its ancient monasteries and palaces. Leh Palace, built in the 17th century, resembles the Potala Palace of Tibet and offers panoramic views of Leh town. Hemis Monastery is the largest and wealthiest monastery in Ladakh, known for its annual festival. Thiksey Monastery, perched on a hilltop, is famous for its large statue of Maitreya Buddha and beautiful architecture.
