Wildlife of Himachal Pradesh

The state is rich in biodiversity, especially due to its location in the Himalayas. Great Himalayan National Park (a UNESCO World Heritage Site) is home to snow leopards, blue sheep, Himalayan tahr, and over 180 species of birds. Pin Valley National Park in the cold desert area supports rare flora and fauna. Renuka Wildlife Sanctuary is a smaller but important sanctuary centered around the sacred Renuka Lake.
