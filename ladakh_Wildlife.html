Wildlife of Ladakh

Ladakh's wildlife is uniquely adapted to its cold desert climate, featuring rare species like the snow leopard, Tibetan antelope, and Himalayan blue sheep. The region's high-altitude national parks and reserves provide a vital sanctuary for these endangered animals..
