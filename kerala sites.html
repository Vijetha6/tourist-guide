Historical Sites of Kerala

Kerala has a rich historical legacy that reflects its interactions with various cultures over centuries. The Padmanabhapuram Palace showcases exquisite wooden architecture from the Travancore era. Bekal Fort, standing along the Arabian Sea, offers breathtaking views and a glimpse into Kerala’s military past. Mattancherry Palace, also known as the Dutch Palace, contains beautiful murals and artifacts from Kochi’s royal history.

Padmanabhapuram Palace

A beautiful wooden palace near Thiruvananthapuram, known for its intricate carvings and murals.

Fort Kochi

Rich in colonial history, Fort Kochi features Portuguese, Dutch, and British influence.
