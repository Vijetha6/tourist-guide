HIMACHAL PRADESH

Himachal Pradesh is a northern Indian state known for its Himalayan landscapes, hill stations, and spiritual retreats.

Geography and Climate

  • Location: Himachal Pradesh is located in northern India, bordered by Jammu & Kashmir, Punjab, Haryana, and Uttarakhand.
  • Capital: Shimla is the summer capital, while Dharamshala is the winter capital.
  • Terrain: It is mountainous, with valleys, rivers, forests, and snow-capped peaks.

Culture and Heritage

  • Language: Hindi is the official language; local dialects like Pahari are also spoken.
  • Cuisine: Includes dishes like siddu, dham, and madra, typically made with grains and local vegetables.
  • Festivals: Celebrates Dussehra, Shivratri, and local fairs like Kullu Dussehra.
