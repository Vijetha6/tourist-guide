Historical Sites of Meghalaya

Meghalaya has cultural heritage sites like Nartiang Monoliths and sacred groves that reflect the region's ancient traditions.

Nartiang Monoliths

Located in the Jaintia Hills, the Nartiang Monoliths are ancient stone structures that were erected by the Jaintia kings. These stand as symbols of Meghalaya's rich past.

Shillong Cathedral

The Cathedral of Mary Help of Christians in Shillong is a historic church with beautiful architecture and serene surroundings.
